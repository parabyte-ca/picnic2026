Location

Getting There

Bronte Creek Provincial Park — 1219 Burloak Drive, Oakville, ON L6M 4J2

Open larger map ↗
EAST

From the East

Coming from Toronto / Mississauga

  1. Take the QEW West toward Hamilton
  2. Take the Burloak Drive exit
  3. Turn Right (North) onto Burloak Drive
  4. Continue north approx. 3.5 km
  5. Park entrance on your left (brown provincial park signs)
  6. At the gate, follow signs to the Day Use Area
  7. Head to the Baseball A Pavilion
WEST

From the West

Coming from Hamilton / Burlington

  1. Take the QEW East toward Toronto
  2. Take the Burloak Drive exit
  3. Turn Left (North) onto Burloak Drive
  4. Continue north approx. 3.5 km
  5. Park entrance on your left (brown provincial park signs)
  6. At the gate, follow signs to the Day Use Area
  7. Head to the Baseball A Pavilion
A daily vehicle permit is required to enter the park. Permits are available at the entrance gate. Parking permit is included with overnight camping.
